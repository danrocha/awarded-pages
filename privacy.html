Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we may also receive your name.

Movie Preferences: We store your watchlist, watched movies, ratings, liked movies, and people you follow.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics about how you use the app to improve your experience.

Technical Data: We collect device information, IP address, and error logs to fix bugs and improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • Sync your movie collections across devices
  • Personalize your experience
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how features are used

Data Storage

On Your Device: The movie and awards database is stored locally on your device for fast, offline access.

In the Cloud: Your account information and movie collections are synced securely via Supabase servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

Supabase Authentication & cloud sync PostHog Analytics Sentry Error tracking Apple Sign-In Google Sign-In TMDb Movie data

Data Security

  • Secure authentication with OAuth and encrypted tokens
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Local data stored securely on your device

Your Rights

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
